HOW TO PLAY
AIM: Move mouse over table to aim
SHOOT: Click and drag away from cue ball, then release. Drag distance = power
SPIN: Click and drag on the spin control (bottom left) to apply english/topspin/backspin
BALL IN HAND: After a foul, click on the table to place the cue ball, then click to confirm
RULES: Standard 8-ball. Pocket all your balls (solids/stripes) then sink the 8-ball to win. Scratching or hitting the wrong ball first is a foul.
